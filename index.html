About Us

Established on March 14, 2015, coincidentally celebrated as 'Pi' day (3.14), KeyGen Coder has evolved from a modest initiative into a formidable organization, thanks to the leadership of Sushobon Gosh, Ratul Bhattarchya, and Soumyadeep Roy. Marking its inception with the first official contest on March 27, 2015, the club has fostered a legacy of success. Despite the challenges posed by the pandemic, KeyGen Coder remains steadfast in its commitment to nurturing problem solvers and coders. Members have achieved remarkable success, securing coveted positions at top companies. Looking ahead, the club aspires to broaden its horizons, introducing new departments and opportunities for members to learn and excel. Aspiring coders and problem solvers are warmly invited to join and contribute to the enduring legacy of KeyGen Coder.

KeyGEn Family

In KeyGenCoders, it's like a big coding family. We all work together to solve problems and write cool code. Imagine it's a big kitchen, and everyone has different ingredients and recipes. We share ideas, help each other when we're stuck, and make amazing dishes of code together. It's not just about coding; it's about learning and having fun. In this big coding kitchen, we all bring something special, and that's what makes KeyGenCoders awesome!
